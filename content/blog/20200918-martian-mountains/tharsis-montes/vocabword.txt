Definition: Three large shield volcanoes in the Tharsis region of the planet Mars. From north to south, the volcanoes are Ascraeus Mons, Pavonis Mons and Arsia Mons. <em>Mons</em> (plural <em>montes</em>) is the Latin word for mountain. <em>Definition is my own, informed by <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tharsis_Montes" target="_blank">Wikipedia</a>.</em>
