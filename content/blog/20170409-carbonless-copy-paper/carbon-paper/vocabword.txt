Vocabword: carbon paper

----

Pronunciation: vəˈrandə

----

Audiopronunciation: 

----

Definition: originally "carbonic" paper, this is a paper coated on one side with a layer of a loosely bound dry ink or pigmented coating, bound with wax, used for making one or more copies simultaneously with the creation of an original document when using a typewriter or a ballpoint pen
